# Funding

There are a number of funding opportunities available to community members based in the UK. Many of these can be found on the [CAKE website](https://www.cake.ac.uk), which has [a dedicated page for funding opportunities for dRTPs and more](https://www.cake.ac.uk/funds/).

## Placements and exchanges

CCP-AHC is particularly interested in supporting short-term placements with and exchanges betweeen other projects working to increase the engagement by arts, humanities, and culture researchers with digital research infrastructures. If you are interested in collaborating on a placement or exchange, please contact [ccpahc@durham.ac.uk](mailto:ccpahc@durham.ac.uk).

Some open funding streams that support placements and exchanges include:

- [CoSeC Funding for Community Collaboration Visits](https://www.cosec.ac.uk/funding/cosec-community-funding-for-collaboration-visits/) (CCP-AHC community members only, [please enquire](mailto:ccpahc@durham.ac.uk))
- [Computational Abilities Knowledge Exchange (CAKE) - Placement & Visits](https://www.cake.ac.uk/about/placements/)
- [ATRIUM Transnational Access (TNA) Individual Access](https://www.atrium-research.eu/tna-individual-access/)
